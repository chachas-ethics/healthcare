Call Emergency Now

When to Visit the Emergency Department

Severe Pain

Sudden chest pain, abdominal pain, or intense headaches.

Accidents & Trauma

Road accidents, falls, fractures, or severe injuries.

Breathing Problems

Difficulty breathing, asthma attacks, or choking.

Uncontrolled Bleeding

Heavy bleeding that does not stop.

Loss of Consciousness

Fainting, seizures, or sudden collapse.

High Fever / Infection

Persistent high fever or signs of severe infection.

What to Do in an Emergency

  1. Stay calm and ensure immediate safety
  2. Call our emergency line or arrive immediately
  3. Provide basic first aid if trained
  4. Bring identification if possible
